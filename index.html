Uganda

Uganda is a landlocked country located in East Africa, known for its rich culture, beautiful landscapes, and diverse wildlife. The country is often called the “Pearl of Africa” because of its natural beauty, which includes lakes, mountains, forests, and national parks. Its capital city is Kampala, which is the largest and busiest city in the country. Uganda’s economy mainly depends on agriculture, tourism, and trade. The country is also famous for attractions such as Bwindi Impenetrable National Park, home to mountain gorillas, and Lake Victoria, Africa’s largest lake.
